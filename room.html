Overview

A warm welcome to the Kingdom of Cambodia. Situated on the banks of the famous Mekong River, the magnificent Hotel Cambodiana is located in the heart of Phnom Penh’s central business and tourist districts, offering visitors convenient access to all that Phnom Penh has to offer.

Hotel Cambodiana is a 4-star hotel that offers a range of amenities and services to ensure a comfortable stay for its guests. The hotel features spacious and well-appointed rooms, a restaurant serving local and international cuisine, a fitness center, and an outdoor swimming pool. Guests can also enjoy complimentary Wi-Fi access throughout the property.

With its prime location, Hotel Cambodiana is an ideal choice for both business and leisure travelers. The hotel is within walking distance of popular attractions such as the Royal Palace, Silver Pagoda, and the bustling riverside promenade. Whether you're visiting for business or pleasure, Hotel Cambodiana provides a welcoming atmosphere and excellent service to make your stay memorable.
